Invesment Banking Intern

For my summer internship at Unified in New York, I had the opportunity to spend my mornings at Cyndx to complete a few projects.

I had three main projects I worked on for Unified, which were creating a waterfall analysis, business valuation, and enterprise comparable company deck. The waterfall was a unique project that modeled out who would be paid out in what preference in the case of an IPO or buyout. I read through documents regarding preferred stocks, debt financings, options, and warrants. The waterfall then inputs a purchase price and shows any preferred to common conversions, warrant or option conversions to common, and gives a detailed payout per person using the treasury stock method. The business valuation includes competitors and their relative revenue metrics. It links the Unified financial model with a DCF analysis, which ends up giving an enterprise value. For the enterprise comps, I went through competitor 10K and 10Q filings to display important metrics per competitors to be compared with Unified.

I also had the opportunity to work with a Cyndx client and learn about Capital IQ. I used searches to pull relative transactions and competitors. Here I also did a WACC calculation.

Thank you to the bankers at Cyndx who worked with me this summer, it was a great learning experience and I took a lot away from these projects.
