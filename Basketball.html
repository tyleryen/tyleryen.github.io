My Career

Basketball has always been a passion of mine since kindergarten. I grew up playing on a rec team called SASC, then played in the Redmond Select program, as well as Pro Club AAU teams. I have two years of high school varsity experience. Now at Santa Clara, I continue to play pick-up, intramurals, and club.

Redmond High School

I had the privilege of playing two years of varsity basketball for my high school. My senior year I was nominated as a captain by my teammates, and started as a guard. I would like to thank Coach Lund and my teammates for an amazing experience, Go Stangs!

South Bay Hoopers

A group of guys at Santa Clara University, myself included, are driven by our love for basketball and are in the process of getting a club team sanctioned by the University (like SCU water polo, rugby, volleyball, etc.). This year, we took the initiative to bring together a group that is playing under the name of South Bay Hoopers. We play in tournaments with other club teams, and have traveled to Cal Poly SLO, Stanford, Cal State Northridge, and play regularly in a competitive San Jose City League.

Spring quarter we plan on having more meetings with the club director at Santa Clara, and are on track to be official next year.
